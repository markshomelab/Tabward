# Privacy Policy for Tabward

**Last updated: May 12, 2026**

## Overview

Tabward is a Chrome extension that automatically brings new tabs into focus when they open. This privacy policy explains what data the extension does and does not collect.

## Data Collection

Tabward collects no data of any kind. It does not:

- Track your browsing activity
- Read the contents of your tabs
- Store any personal information
- Transmit any data to any server
- Use analytics or tracking tools of any kind

## Permissions

Tabward requests two permissions:

- `tabs` — required to detect when a new tab is opened. This permission is not used to read URLs, titles, or any other tab content.
- `storage` — required to remember whether you have the extension turned on or off. This preference is stored locally on your device and never transmitted anywhere.

## Third Parties

Tabward does not share any data with third parties because it does not collect any data in the first place.
